About

Design fluency,
field experience

DeepRoot Design is owned and operated by Sydney Szypszak — a designer with a background in architecture and construction management who found her true passion in the landscape.

With formal training in Revit and hands-on experience working under a licensed landscape architect, Sydney brings something rare to residential landscape design: the precision of an architect paired with the knowledge of someone who has worked the soil.

The result is design work that looks professional, holds up to HOA scrutiny, and translates seamlessly into real installations.

How it works

From photos to finished plan

01
You share the details

Send photos of your property and your address. For remote projects, that's all we need to get started.

02
We build the model

Your home and existing landscape are modeled in Revit — accurately, to scale, with real dimensions.

03
Design is developed

Proposed plantings, hardscape, and layout are designed and layered into the model with full plant specifications.

04
Package delivered

You receive a professional document set — plans, elevations, renderings, and plant schedule — ready for HOA or contractor use.
